About

I was born in Milan on August 13th, 1991. When I was a child, I had a passion for Pokemon video games on the Nintendo Game Boy, and I dreamed of becoming a programmer so that I could create my own games. At 14, I started to learn HTML and CSS, but at that age, I had not yet developed the ability to tolerate frustration. As a result, I soon gave up on everything. However, this did not stop me from pursuing my passions and ambitions in other ways.
In 2007, YouTube appeared in my life, and I began to take an interest in content creation by watching and learning everything I could.
I have fulfilled my dream of graduating in Mathematics. That same year, I became aware of NFTs and immediately wanted to learn more. In general, I was positively impressed by the world of distributed ledgers and Web3. I regularly participate in events and talks on these topics and try to stay as up-to-date as possible on developments in the field.
My choices have taken me far from what I dreamed of when I was little, but I am very proud and grateful for my journey.
Currently, I manage and create content for my YouTube channel, enjoy web development, and work as a Math tutor.
